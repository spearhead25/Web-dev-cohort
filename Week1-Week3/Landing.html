WELCOME TO THE ULTIMATE BATTLE

Three houses compete for ultimate glory and yet again we are back for the season of high adrenaline and tough battles. The matches will be held with each team playing agains each other once and the top two teams making it to the final. The stakes are high and here we are for another gripping tournament that is going to take you all at the edge of your seats.

THE HOUSES

INHUMANE

We are known as the house of aggression. We are the powerhouse of representing sheer strength in our character that sometimes in critical situations, we possess super natural qualities to assert dominance on the opponent. We transform our abilities in a very intense way due to which we have derived the title of "INHUMANES".

BEAST

We are known as the house of demons. Whenever we step out there in the field, we aim to consistently put pressure on the opponent so that they are unable to find loopholes. We are always known to not leave any loose ends to the opponents and we are always ready to attack with pure animal power. That makes us earn the title of "BEASTS".

INDOMITABLE

We are known as the house of resilience. We refuse to give up in any situation and fight till the last drop of our blood. Even if we are sure of losing, we give our all till our last soldier is left, because it's never over for us until it's officially over. That's why we are known for our ultimate comebacks and grit which makes us "INDOMITABLES".

SPEARHEADS

We are known as the house of equal power. We are more of an attacking team and we don't have a leader, we all equally lead from the front leaving no bias. We believe that increases our strength on the field as no one feels inferior and everyone's ready to give it their all. This makes us a brutally attacking team which has earned us the title of "SPEARHEADS".

IMMORTALS

We are known as the house of danger. With our arrival we make sure that mentally we have already won half the battle. We represent ourselves in such a way that the opponents feel more alerted with their troops. This gives us a mental edge to us because of variety of power distribution in our soldiers. We are known for our sheer mental edge which makes us "IMMORTALS".
